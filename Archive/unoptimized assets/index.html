My Work

Check out some of my latest work below

UniChat

Chat Beyond Boundaries

A MEAN stack web app which lets users chat with each other either or in a group either privately or publicly in their native language. With a choice of 15 languages to choose from, chatting especially with a multicultural crowd has never been easier. Messages are translated as per user's preferred language and have associated sentiment analysis alongside to give a better idea of the tone of the message as translations today aren't perfect.

Infographer

An easy to use infographic creator

A RESTful MEAN stack web application that lets users easily create, save, print and share infographics. Use one of the available backgrounds, text, shapes and images from Flickr or upload your own and customize. Supports Google, Facebook and email sign up with password recovery using Twilio SMS. Save infographics to your Dropbox or computer, share on Facebook or just print it and stick it on the notice board
